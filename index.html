About Me

"Hello, dear friends"

For those who don't know me, I'm a simple gummy bear who skyrocketed to fame 16 years ago; Definitely a wonderful time, full of sensations of ecstasy and euphoria in an endless loop. However, fame was short-lived, money ran out, and the fans and millions of hearts that adored me worldwide had forgotten about me, while I hadn't learned to live without them. This is my sad story; after years of alcoholism, failures, unenjoyable drug addictions... just when I thought I couldn't sink any lower, I took to dancing a tango with death, only to stumble on the final step before feeling the cold scythe snatch my soul away. A last chance, a revelation, like a diamond in the darkness, I began to shine. Slowly, I started to dabble in Twitter, Telegram, but what can I tell you about crypto that you don't already know. That's right, crypto has saved my life, and now I'm back, more movin', groovin', jammin', singing gummy bear than ever.
